01 Who We Are

The team behind Kreeda TV

We are Kreeda TV ("Developer," "we," "our," or "us"), an independent mobile application developer publishing sports-focused streaming and entertainment applications on the Google Play Store. Our apps are built for fans who want fast, reliable access to live sports content and related media.

This Privacy Policy governs the Kreeda TV application and any companion apps we publish. By installing and using Kreeda TV, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 Scope

Where this policy applies

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by Kreeda TV on the Google Play Store, including:

  • Live sports streaming and IPTV playback (M3U playlist players, channel guides)
  • Sports highlights, scores, and entertainment apps
  • Companion utilities, media tools, and related products
  • Any current or future apps we release under the Kreeda TV name

Where a specific app has unique privacy considerations, we may supplement this policy with an in-app notice. Read this policy as the baseline for everything we publish.

03 Data We Collect

What we collect — and what we don't

We collect only the minimum data necessary. The exact data depends on the app and the features you use:

  • Device Information: Device model, manufacturer, Android OS version, screen resolution, language, and app version — used for compatibility and bug fixing.
  • Usage & Analytics Data: Screens visited, features used, session duration, and button interactions — collected anonymously to understand how the app is used.
  • Crash & Error Reports: Automatically generated logs when the app crashes, including device state and error stack trace — used to fix bugs. No personal data is included.
  • App-Specific User Data (Local Only): Data you enter inside the app (URLs, playlist links, favorites, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We cannot access it.
  • Network Information: Connection type (Wi-Fi or mobile data) and general IP address — used only for optimizing content delivery and not retained beyond the active session.
  • Permissions-Based Data: If the app requests device permissions (storage, network state, etc.), it will only access that data when you use a feature that requires it. Permission access is always opt-in.
What we do NOT collect: your name, email address, phone number, payment information, contacts, messages, or any other personally identifiable information — unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting our support.
04 How We Use Data

What we do with what we collect

Data we collect is used exclusively for these purposes:

  • App Operation: To provide the core features and functionality of Kreeda TV.
  • Performance Optimization: To detect and fix crashes, reduce buffering, and improve playback stability.
  • User Experience: To remember your settings, preferences, and in-app configurations between sessions.
  • Product Improvement: To understand which features are most useful using anonymous, aggregated analytics.
  • Support: If you contact us, we use the information you voluntarily share (device details, error descriptions) to resolve your issue.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our obligations under applicable laws and regulations.

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, behavioral profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating and improving the app.

05 Storage & Retention

Where data lives and for how long

  • Local Device Storage: User-generated data (settings, configurations, favorites, history) is stored on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Analytics & Crash Data: Anonymous diagnostic data is retained by third-party analytics services (see Section 7) for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Support Communications: If you email us for support, messages are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your issue, and no longer than 12 months.
  • Deletion on Uninstall: All locally stored app data is permanently removed from your device when you uninstall Kreeda TV.

We do not operate a server-side user database. We do not store personal profiles or accounts associated with you.

06 Data Sharing

Who else sees your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Data may only be shared in these strictly limited circumstances:

  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized, aggregated usage data with services like Google Firebase Analytics. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Crash Reporting Services: Automated error logs (no personal data) with services like Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix technical issues.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or government request, or to enforce our Terms of Service or protect user safety.
  • Business Transfer: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, user data may transfer to the new entity with at least 30 days' prior notice to users.
Third-party content: Kreeda TV may connect directly to third-party servers based on URLs, playlists, or links you provide. Those connections are governed by the third party's own privacy policy — not ours.

08 Data Security

How we protect your data

We take reasonable and industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data sent between the app and external services uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Local Data Encryption: Sensitive locally stored data (such as credentials) uses Android's encrypted SharedPreferences or Keystore where applicable.
  • Minimal Data Collection: The less data we collect, the less there is to protect. We follow a strict data minimization approach.
  • No Central Database: Since we do not maintain a user database, there is no central server holding your personal information that could be compromised.
  • Regular Updates: We release app updates to patch security vulnerabilities. Keep the app updated for best protection.

No digital system can guarantee 100% security. We encourage you to protect your device with a screen lock and exercise caution when granting app permissions.

09 Your Rights

Your rights and choices

You have full control over your data. Here's what you can do at any time:

  • Clear App Data: Android Settings → Apps → Kreeda TV → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored data.
  • Revoke Permissions: Manage storage, network, or other permissions via Android Settings → Apps → Kreeda TV → Permissions.
  • Opt Out of Analytics: Kreeda TV may include a Settings → Privacy toggle to disable anonymous analytics collection.
  • Opt Out of Ad Personalization: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app permanently deletes all local data immediately.
  • Data Deletion Request: For any data you voluntarily shared with us (via support email), contact us and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.

Users in certain regions (EU/EEA, California, India, etc.) may have additional rights such as data portability and the right to object to processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10 Children's Privacy

Protections for younger users

Kreeda TV is intended for users who are at least 13 years old (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages.

Some content available through Kreeda TV may not be suitable for younger viewers, depending on external sources connected by the user. Parents are encouraged to supervise usage and utilize Android's built-in parental controls.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at minovalabs@gmail.com and we will promptly investigate and delete any such information.
